History

TechCon was established in 2010 as a premier global technology conference to bring together thought leaders, innovators, and enthusiasts from various tech disciplines. Over the years, TechCon has evolved into a hub for showcasing cutting-edge innovations, discussing emerging trends, and fostering collaboration among tech professionals. It has grown from a regional event to a globally recognized conference, attracting participants from over 50 countries.
